Winning Strategy

Mindset, technique & execution — how toppers crack GPSC COS

🎯 Attempt Strategy in Exam

1

First 60 min — Easy Pass

Attempt all questions you know 100% — skip doubts. Target 80–90 easy Qs.

2

Next 60 min — Educated Guesses

Return to medium-difficulty Qs. Eliminate 2 options → 50/50 chance. Take calculated risks.

3

Next 45 min — Tough Questions

Attempt only if you can eliminate 1-2 options. Never leave anything blank if no negative marking confirmed.

4

Last 15 min — Review

Revisit marked questions. Check OMR sheet carefully. No changing instinctive answers.

💡 Power Tips for Saurabh

01

Gujarat = Double Marks

Every Gujarat-specific topic appears in BOTH static GK and Current Affairs sections. Mastering Gujarat = maximising score with minimum effort.

02

One-Liner Notes System

After studying each topic, write 10 one-liners. Review these every Sunday. By exam day, you'll have 450+ facts at fingertips.

03

Mock Tests Are Non-Negotiable

3 full mocks minimum in last 10 days. Timed. Strict. Analyse every wrong answer. Mock scores are NOT marks — analysis is.

04

Sleep = Study

Your brain consolidates memory during sleep. 7 hours is mandatory. Sleep deprivation kills recall ability in exams.

05

Previous Papers Are Gold

GPSC COS tends to repeat topic patterns. Solve last 3 years' papers and you'll spot the high-frequency zones clearly.

06

No New Topics After May 27

Last 10 days = revision only. Starting new topics in final week creates confusion and damages confidence. Stick to revision.
